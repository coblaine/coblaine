Background

I'm a game designer, currently based in Cincinnati, OH. I work with a wide variety of clients, from individuals looking for someone to build their dream game to independent studios and startups that need a talented professional to help to develop their projects. I graduated from Miami University, with a bachelors in Interactive Media Studies.

I've spent my university years studying many aspects of video game developement, even taking numerous classes on Computer Science to help bolster my knowledge of programming. The time spent learning reignited my passion for game developement. The feeling of creating an interactive experience that people get to spend time enjoying and obtaining the same joy I felt playing games as a child is astounding. I'd love to work with good people to extend this excitement to many different audiences.

If I'm not working hard on a job, I'm probably working on a personal project, playing video games or spending time building model kits.
